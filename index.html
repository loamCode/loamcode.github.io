Thomas Art Burke, MPH

Food Systems Researcher | Planetary Health for the Future

Previously a specialist in digital food traceability systems, I research agri-food systems and their contributions to plantary changes. In particular, I am interested in quantifying economic and environmental tradeoffs from production modifications in animal agriculture. Through elucidating the causal underpinnings of One Health, we may be able to find equilibrium between man-made and natural biological systems.
